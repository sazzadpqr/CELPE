APP PURPOSE

================================================================
CelpePrep is a Brazilian Portuguese learning and Celpe-Bras preparation app.
It must help users:
- prepare for Celpe-Bras
- practice Tarefa 1, Tarefa 2, Tarefa 3, Tarefa 4, and oral tasks
- take AI-generated Celpe-Bras style practices
- receive AI feedback based on their actual writing
- learn Brazilian Portuguese vocabulary deeply
- review vocabulary with SRS flashcards
- study grammar, reading, listening, pronunciation, writing, and conversation
- access previous Celpe-Bras exam papers/materials
- study manual non-AI materials
- follow an adaptive study plan
- track progress
- use premium features after payment
- allow admin to control almost every piece of content and configuration
The app must be useful as a website first.
It must also be compatible with future Android/iOS conversion through safe adapter patterns.

- expires_at timestamptz not null

- request_ip_hash text nullable
- user_agent_hash text nullable
OTP rules:
- 6 numeric digits
- expires in 10 minutes
- max 5 wrong attempts
- max 3 sends per email per 15 minutes
- max 5 sends per email per day
- store only hashed OTP
- never expose account existence
- consumed after success
- cannot reuse OTP
Development-safe OTP:
Environment:
EMAIL_PROVIDER = mock/resend/sendgrid/smtp
OTP_DEV_MODE = true/false
OTP_DEV_CODE = 123456

- do not crash
- generate/store OTP
- return devMode true
- frontend shows:
"Modo teste: use o código 123456"
